Privacy Policy for the Zood Food App

Effective Date: July 2, 2025

Your trust is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how the Zood Food app (referred to as "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Technical information: device type, model, operating system, language, app version.
  • Location data (if permitted): to suggest the nearest restaurants.
  • Personal information: name, phone number, delivery address, and email.
  • Order information: list of items ordered, total price, and order time.

2. How We Use the Information

We use your information to:

  • process and deliver your orders;
  • improve our services and app experience;
  • contact you regarding your orders;
  • analyze and enhance app functionality;
  • comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • to fulfill your order (e.g., delivery providers);
  • if required by law or regulation;
  • with your explicit consent.

4. Data Storage and Protection

  • We take reasonable security measures to protect your data.
  • Your information is stored on secure servers and is not accessed unnecessarily.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • request access to your data;
  • request correction or deletion of your data;
  • withdraw consent for data processing (note: this may affect app functionality).

To exercise your rights, contact us at: parniyonteam@gmail.com

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (e.g., maps or analytics), which may also collect data. Please refer to their privacy policies for more details.

7. Policy Updates

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be communicated through the app or our website.
